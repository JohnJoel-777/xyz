Story 1 : Minnu learns something new

Once,in a village, there lived a bee
named Minnu.One day , Minnu Bee went
out to collect nectar from the flowers.
Just then, she saw Krowy crow pecking
Zoya Cow's back.

" Gosh ! Someone
please come here
and help ! Look ! Krowy Crow
is pecking Zoya Cow." Zoya cow suddenly

turned around , and said
to Minnu , " Pal , Krowy is
not trying to hurt me. He
is my good friend."
" Then , why is he pecking
you ? " asked Minnu curiously.

Zoya smiled at Minnu and
said , " Friend , whenever some bugs
sit on my back and irritate
me , I call Krowy Crow.
He comes , pecks and removes
the bugs from my back.
He is actually helping
me to clean my body."

"Oh , is that so ? That's really
amazing ! " Minnu
exclaimed in wonder , and went
on her way happily.
